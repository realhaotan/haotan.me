Welcome to my blog. I plan to use this space to share thoughts on my research in deep model fusion and multi-task learning, interesting technical problems I encounter, and reflections on the academic journey.

## Why start a blog?

Writing helps me think more clearly. By putting ideas into words, I often discover gaps in my understanding that I wouldn't have noticed otherwise. I also hope these posts might be useful to others working in similar areas.

## What to expect

I'll be writing about:

- Research insights and paper reviews
- Technical tutorials and code walkthroughs
- Reflections on the PhD experience

Stay tuned for more.
