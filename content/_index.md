I am a PhD candidate in the School of Computer Science at the [University of Leeds](https://eps.leeds.ac.uk/computer-science), UK. My research explores how deep learning models can be more effectively combined, adapted, and reused across different vision tasks. My current work focuses on fusion strategies for deep model fusion and multi-task learning.

Beyond research, I am interested in technology, investment, and understanding complex systems through multidisciplinary models. I enjoy exploring how insights from different fields can be brought together to better explain real-world problems and inform decision-making.
